of PDD Holdings. &ldquo;As a public platform, we will continue to invest in merchant support initiatives, driving industry upgrades and
the sustainable development of the platform in the long run.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&ldquo;In the third quarter, revenues growth

Liu, VP of Finance of PDD Holdings. &ldquo;As we roll out greater merchant support initiatives and ecosystem investments, financial results
may continue to fluctuate from quarter to quarter.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Third Quarter 2025 Unaudited Financial Results</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>Total revenues </B>were RMB108,276.5 million
(US$15,209.5 million), an increase of 9% from RMB99,354.4 million in the same quarter of 2024. The increase was primarily due to the
increase in revenues from online marketing services and transaction services.</P>


<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; width: 100%"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top; text-align: justify">

                                                                                (US$7,493.7 million), an increase of 8% from RMB49,351.0 million in the same quarter of 2024.</TD>
</TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; width: 100%"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top; text-align: justify">

                                                                                increase of 10% compared with RMB50,003.4 million in the same quarter of 2024.</TD>
</TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Total costs of revenues</B> were RMB46,840.2 million (US$6,579.6
million), an increase of 18% from RMB39,709.2 million in the same quarter of 2024. The increase mainly came from the increased fulfilment
fees, bandwidth and server costs, and payment processing fees.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Total operating expenses</B> were RMB36,410.4 million (US$5,114.5
million), compared with RMB35,352.7 million in the same quarter of 2024.</P>
